# **App Name**: Cosmic Compass

## Core Features:

- Birth Data Input: Collect user's birth details (date, time, location).
- Monthly Forecast: Predict astrological insights for the current and upcoming month, focusing on major transits and their potential effects. Uses an AI tool to selectively incorporate traditional astrological interpretations.
- Yearly Outlook: Provide brief yearly astrological overviews, highlighting key planetary influences and potential life themes. Uses an AI tool to selectively incorporate traditional astrological interpretations.
- Event Prediction: Predict significant upcoming life events based on astrological charts and transits. Uses an AI tool to selectively incorporate traditional astrological interpretations.
- Palm Image Upload: Allow users to upload a palm image for analysis.
- Palm Reading: Analyze the uploaded palm image and generate a personalized reading based on traditional palmistry principles. Uses an AI tool to selectively incorporate traditional palmistry interpretations.
- Report Display: Present the astrological predictions and palmistry readings in an easy to understand and visually appealing format.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Deep Indigo (#4B0082) to evoke a sense of mystery and the cosmos.
- Background color: Very dark desaturated indigo (#1A1A2E), close to black, for a dark theme.
- Accent color: Violet (#8F00FF) for interactive elements and highlights.
- Headline font: 'Belleza' (sans-serif) for a stylish and personalized feel. Body font: 'Alegreya' (serif) for body, to promote readability in longer passages.
- Use star-shaped and line icons to represent astrological elements.
- Subtle animations when the A.I. is analyzing your data or when navigating through predictions.
